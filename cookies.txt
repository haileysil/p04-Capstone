Ingredients
1 cup butter
0.75 cup whitesugar
0.75 cup brownsugar
1 tsp vanilla
2 whole eggs
2.25 cups flour
1 tsp bakingsoda
0.5 tsp salt
2 cups chocolatechips
1 cup nuts(optional)

Directions
Heat oven to 375. Beat butter, sugars, and vanilla. Add eggs. Beat mixture well.Stir in flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add to butter mixture. Stir in chocolate chips and nuts. Drop onto cookie sheet. Bake 8-10 minutes or until golden brown. Cool slightly before removing from cookie sheet. Cool completely before storing.

Enjoy!
