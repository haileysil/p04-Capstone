1 can enchiladasauce
2.5 cups chicken
1.5 cups cheese
1 package seasoning
10 whole tortillas

# Directions
Heat oven to 375. Spray pan with cooking spray. Spread 0.75 cups sauce on the bottom of the pan. In medium bowl, mix chicken, cheese, and seasoning. Fill each tortilla with about half a cup of the mixture. Roll up each tortilla and place seam side down in the pan. Drizzle remaining sauce and cheese on the enchiladas.cover with foil and bake for 15 minutes. Uncover and bake for 15 minutes longer until slightly brown. Cool slightly before eating and cool completely before storing.

Enjoy!
