1.5 pounds beef
1 whole egg
1 whole onion
1 cup milk
0.5 tsp salt
0.5 tsp pepper
2 tbsp mustard
0.25 cup ketchup
1 cup breadcrumbs

#Directions
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Combine beef, egg, onion, milk and breadcrumbs in a bowl. Form the mixture into a loaf shape and place into a loaf pan. In a seperate bowl, combine the brown sugar, mustard and ketchup. Mix well and pour over the meatloaf. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour. Makes sure to test the internal temperature before taking the loaf out of the oven. Let cool slightly before eating and cool completely before storing.

Enjoy!
